About

About the event

Earn and Sell, organized by the Entrepreneurship Development Cell at VIT Pune, is an event that replicates real-world business scenarios.

  • The event comprises two phases, starting with an initial pitching round to secure investments from the E-Cell for qualifying teams.
  • The funds provided by the E-Cell are then utilized by these teams to operate an actual business within the college for a day.
  • Prior to the competition rounds, participants will attend a college business workshop, during which they will receive guidance on effectively presenting their ideas to potential investors.
